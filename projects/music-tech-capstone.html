Depth-Controlled Spatial Audio with High Order Ambisonics in a Speaker Ring

Music and Technology Capstone Project

For my Music and Technology degree, I completed an individual "Capstone-like" project that combined music with technology. This project is a system that incorporates depth-tracking and ambisonic audio to allow the user to accurately control an audio source's origin and intensity in a circular plane, as produced by a 360° speaker ring.
